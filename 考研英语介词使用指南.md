# 考研英语介词使用指南：从本质到搭配

> 核心原则：介词不是一个个孤立的“中文意思”，而是英语用来表达“关系”的小词。考研英语里，介词主要负责说明：两个名词之间是什么关系、动作朝向哪里、信息从哪里来、影响作用到谁、某事发生在什么范围或过程中。

## 一、先理解：介词的本质是什么

中文常靠语序和语境表达关系，英语更依赖介词把关系说清楚。

比如中文说：

```text
教育的重要性
对社会的影响
从经验中学习
在压力下保持冷静
用证据质疑假设
```

英语必须把关系具体化：

```text
the importance of education
an impact on society
learn from experience
remain calm under pressure
challenge assumptions with evidence
```

所以，介词的本质不是“翻译成某个中文词”，而是回答下面这些问题：

```text
A 属于 B 吗？           -> of
A 指向 B 吗？           -> to
A 作用到 B 上吗？       -> on
A 从 B 来吗？           -> from
A 为了 B 吗？           -> for
A 借助 B 完成吗？       -> with / by / through
A 处在 B 的范围里吗？   -> in
A 处在某个点上吗？      -> at
A 反对或对抗 B 吗？     -> against
A 围绕 B 讨论吗？       -> about
```

学习介词最有效的方法是：先抓核心空间感，再理解抽象延伸，最后背高频搭配。

## 二、核心介词详解

## 1. in：在“范围”里面

### 本质

`in` 的核心是“在一个范围、容器、领域、状态、过程里面”。这个范围可以是真实空间，也可以是时间段、社会领域、语境、状态。

### 空间用法

```text
in the room
in China
in the box
```

表示在一个有边界的范围里面。

### 时间用法

```text
in 2026
in May
in the morning
in three years
```

`in` 用于较大的时间范围，或者表示“多久之后”。

### 抽象用法

```text
in modern society
in education
in a meaningful context
in trouble
in the process of learning
```

这些不是物理空间，而是抽象范围。

### 考研高频搭配

```text
in modern society
in a meaningful context
in the process of
in the absence of
in terms of
in contrast to / with
in response to
in relation to
```

### 例句

```text
New words are easier to remember when they appear in meaningful contexts.
```

这里 `in meaningful contexts` 表示“在有意义的语境范围里”。

### 易错点

不要把所有“在”都翻成 `in`。

```text
at 10:00       具体时间点，不用 in
on Monday      具体某一天，不用 in
on the surface 在表面上，不用 in
```

---

## 2. on：接触、作用、关于

### 本质

`on` 的核心是“贴在上面、压在上面、作用到上面”。从空间上的“接触表面”，延伸出“影响作用于某对象”“关注某话题”。

### 空间用法

```text
on the table
on the wall
on the screen
```

表示接触某个表面。

### 时间用法

```text
on Monday
on May 27
on the morning of May 27
```

具体某一天或某一天的上午/下午/晚上用 `on`。

### 抽象用法

```text
focus on the problem
depend on evidence
have an impact on society
a book on education
```

这里的 `on` 表示“关注点/作用对象/话题”。

### 考研高频搭配

```text
focus on
concentrate on
depend on
rely on
have an impact on
have an effect on
comment on
research on
```

### 例句

```text
Digital technology has a significant impact on the way students acquire knowledge.
```

`impact on` 表示影响作用到某对象上。

### 易错点

`affect` 是及物动词，不加 `on`：

```text
Technology affects education.        正确
Technology affects on education.     错误
Technology has an effect on education. 正确
```

---

## 3. at：点

### 本质

`at` 的核心是“点”。它把时间、地点、阶段、状态看成一个点。

### 空间用法

```text
at the gate
at school
at the station
```

强调位置点，不强调内部范围。

### 时间用法

```text
at 10:00
at noon
at night
at the beginning
```

具体时间点用 `at`。

### 抽象用法

```text
at risk
at a high level
at the expense of
be good at English
```

表示处在某个状态点或能力点上。

### 考研高频搭配

```text
at the same time
at first
at least
at risk
at the expense of
at the beginning of
be good at
```

### 例句

```text
Students who rely only on short-term memory are at risk of forgetting key concepts quickly.
```

`at risk of doing` 表示“处于做某事的风险中”。

---

## 4. of：内在所属、内容、性质

### 本质

`of` 是英语里最重要的名词关系介词。它不只是“的”，它表示 A 和 B 之间存在内在联系：所属、内容、部分整体、性质、来源、对象。

### 常见关系

```text
the value of education        教育的价值
a lack of evidence            证据的缺乏
a sense of responsibility     责任感
the role of culture           文化的作用
the result of the policy      政策的结果
```

### 考研高频搭配

```text
the importance of
the role of
the value of
the development of
the result of
the cause of
a lack of
a number of
a variety of
a sense of
```

### 例句

```text
The value of education lies not only in knowledge, but also in the ability to think independently.
```

`the value of education` 表示“价值属于教育这个对象”。

### 易错点

中文“的”不一定都用 `of`。如果表示“某人/某机构拥有”，常用所有格：

```text
students' ability
society's memory
China's development
```

但抽象概念之间的关系常用 `of`：

```text
the development of society
the importance of evidence
```

---

## 5. to：方向、对象、结果、对应

### 本质

`to` 的核心是“朝向”。它可以表示真实方向，也可以表示动作、态度、关系、结果朝向某个对象。

### 方向用法

```text
go to school
move to a city
send a letter to him
```

### 抽象用法

```text
adapt to change
lead to problems
contribute to success
access to education
a solution to the problem
an approach to learning
```

这些都是“朝向某对象/结果/领域”。

### 考研高频搭配

```text
adapt to
lead to
contribute to
respond to
refer to
be related to
be relevant to
be similar to
access to
an approach to
a solution to
the key to
```

### 最重要的语法点：to 不一定是不定式

`to` 有两种身份：

```text
to do      不定式符号
to + 名词/动名词   介词
```

比较：

```text
I want to learn English.          to 是不定式符号
I look forward to learning English. to 是介词
```

考研高频“to 是介词”的结构：

```text
adapt to change
contribute to solving the problem
an approach to learning
be used to doing
look forward to doing
object to doing
```

### 易错点

```text
an approach to learn English      错误
an approach to learning English   正确
```

因为 `approach to` 里的 `to` 是介词，后面接名词或动名词。

---

## 6. for：目的、对象、利益、时间长度

### 本质

`for` 的核心是“为了、面向、服务于”。它表示目的、受益对象、原因、用途、时间长度。

### 常见用法

```text
prepare for the exam
for children
for example
for two years
be responsible for
be necessary for
```

### 考研高频搭配

```text
be responsible for
be necessary for
provide support for
create opportunities for
have a reason for
prepare for
for example
for the purpose of
```

### 例句

```text
Equal access to education creates more opportunities for children from disadvantaged families.
```

`for children` 表示机会面向、服务于孩子。

### for 和 to 的区别

```text
give sth. to sb.      强调方向：把东西给到某人
make sth. for sb.     强调目的/受益者：为某人做某物
```

抽象区别：

```text
a solution to the problem      问题的解决方案，solution 指向 problem
support for students           给学生的支持，support 服务于 students
```

---

## 7. with：伴随、工具、具有

### 本质

`with` 的核心是“带着”。可以表示伴随、工具、材料、特征、对象之间的关联。

### 常见用法

```text
with confidence
with reliable evidence
communicate with others
deal with problems
be associated with
```

### 考研高频搭配

```text
deal with
cope with
communicate with
be associated with
be concerned with
be faced with
with reliable evidence
with the help of
```

### 例句

```text
Real critical thinking requires students to challenge assumptions with reliable evidence.
```

`with reliable evidence` 表示“借助可靠证据”，是工具关系。

### with 和 by 的区别

```text
with evidence     用具体工具/材料
by practice       通过某种方式
through practice  经过某个过程
```

---

## 8. by：方式、手段、执行者

### 本质

`by` 的核心是“靠近某方式/经由某方式”。它常表示手段、方式、被动句中的动作发出者。

### 常见用法

```text
by reading
by accident
by contrast
be influenced by
be caused by
```

### 考研高频搭配

```text
by means of
by contrast
by comparison
be shaped by
be affected by
be caused by
```

### 例句

```text
Language ability can be improved by repeated practice.
```

`by repeated practice` 表示通过某种方式提高。

### by 和 through 的区别

`by` 更强调方法，`through` 更强调过程。

```text
improve writing by reading good essays
通过阅读好文章这种方法提高写作

build confidence through repeated practice
经过反复练习这个过程建立信心
```

---

## 9. from：来源、起点、分离

### 本质

`from` 的核心是“从……来 / 从……分离”。它表示来源、起点、原因、差异、阻止。

### 常见用法

```text
come from
learn from mistakes
benefit from feedback
suffer from anxiety
prevent sb. from doing
```

### 考研高频搭配

```text
benefit from
derive from
learn from
differ from
distinguish A from B
prevent A from doing
protect A from B
```

### 例句

```text
Students can benefit from meaningful feedback only when they are willing to reflect on their mistakes.
```

`benefit from` 表示收益来源。

---

## 10. about：围绕话题；本质是

### 本质

`about` 的核心是“围绕”。它可以表示谈论的话题，也可以表示某事的本质。

### 常见用法

```text
talk about
think about
worry about
be concerned about
bring about change
```

### 考研高频搭配

```text
be concerned about
be worried about
think about
learn about
bring about
not about doing A, but about doing B
```

### 例句

```text
Critical thinking is not about rejecting every opinion, but about testing ideas with evidence.
```

这里 `about` 不译成“关于”，而是表示“本质在于”。

### 语法重点：about 后面接名词或动名词

```text
about evidence
about learning
about testing ideas
```

所以：

```text
not about reject opinions      错误
not about rejecting opinions   正确
```

---

## 11. against：反对、对抗、防范、背景

### 本质

`against` 的核心是“顶着、逆着、对抗”。

### 常见用法

```text
argue against the view
fight against poverty
protect against risk
against the background of social change
```

### 考研高频搭配

```text
argue against
fight against
guard against
protect against
against the background of
```

### 例句

```text
Education can protect young people against narrow thinking.
```

`protect A against B` 表示保护 A 免受 B 的影响。

---

## 12. through：穿过过程，通过经历

### 本质

`through` 的核心是“穿过”。抽象化以后，表示通过一个过程、经历、渠道达到结果。

### 常见用法

```text
through practice
through education
through experience
go through difficulties
```

### 考研高频搭配

```text
through repeated practice
through education
through experience
through communication
go through a difficult period
```

### 例句

```text
Real competence is built through repeated practice rather than sudden inspiration.
```

这里强调能力是经过反复练习这个过程形成的。

## 三、考研最常考的介词搭配组

## 1. to 组：方向和对应

```text
access to education
adapt to change
contribute to progress
lead to problems
respond to pressure
be related to society
be relevant to the issue
be similar to previous cases
an approach to learning
a solution to the problem
the key to success
```

重点：很多 `to` 是介词，后面接名词或动名词。

## 2. on 组：作用对象和关注点

```text
focus on evidence
concentrate on study
depend on context
rely on reliable sources
have an impact on society
have an effect on behavior
comment on the issue
```

重点：`affect` 后面不加 `on`，但 `have an effect on` 要加 `on`。

## 3. from 组：来源和分离

```text
benefit from feedback
derive from experience
learn from mistakes
differ from traditional views
distinguish fact from opinion
prevent students from making the same mistake
protect children from harmful information
```

重点：`prevent A from doing` 里的 `from` 不能漏。

## 4. with 组：工具和关联

```text
deal with pressure
cope with uncertainty
communicate with others
be associated with success
be concerned with the issue
challenge assumptions with reliable evidence
```

重点：`with evidence` 表示“用证据”。

## 5. of 组：名词之间的内在关系

```text
the importance of education
the value of tradition
the role of culture
the cause of the problem
the result of the policy
a lack of resources
a sense of responsibility
```

重点：写作文时，`of` 结构是构造抽象名词短语的核心。

## 6. for 组：目的和对象

```text
be responsible for decisions
be necessary for success
provide support for students
create opportunities for children
prepare for the exam
a reason for failure
```

重点：`for` 常表示“为谁/为什么目的”。

## 四、你现在最该警惕的错误

## 1. 看到 to 就接动词原形

错误：

```text
an approach to learn English
contribute to solve the problem
be used to study late
```

正确：

```text
an approach to learning English
contribute to solving the problem
be used to studying late
```

原因：这些结构里的 `to` 是介词，不是不定式符号。

## 2. 把中文“对……有影响”机械翻译

错误：

```text
Technology affects on education.
```

正确：

```text
Technology affects education.
Technology has an impact on education.
Technology has an effect on education.
```

原因：`affect` 是及物动词，直接接宾语；`impact/effect` 是名词，需要 `on` 表示作用对象。

## 3. 忘记固定介词

错误：

```text
adapt change
benefit feedback
prevent students making mistakes
```

正确：

```text
adapt to change
benefit from feedback
prevent students from making mistakes
```

原因：这些动词和介词共同构成固定关系。

## 4. 介词后误接动词原形

错误：

```text
about reject opinions
without understand the context
by read books
```

正确：

```text
about rejecting opinions
without understanding the context
by reading books
```

原因：介词后面接名词、代词或动名词，不接动词原形。

## 五、一个总规则：先判断关系，再选择介词

做翻译时不要先想“这个中文词对应哪个介词”，而要先问关系：

```text
在某个范围里？       -> in
具体点？             -> at
作用到某对象上？     -> on
内在所属或内容？     -> of
朝向对象/结果？      -> to
为了某对象/目的？    -> for
借助工具或伴随？     -> with
通过方式？           -> by
经过过程？           -> through
来自某来源？         -> from
围绕某话题？         -> about
反对/防范？          -> against
```

例：

```text
用可靠证据质疑未经检验的假设
```

分析关系：

```text
质疑什么？ assumptions
用什么质疑？ reliable evidence
```

所以：

```text
challenge untested assumptions with reliable evidence
```

再比如：

```text
从错误中学习
```

分析关系：

```text
学习的来源是什么？ mistakes
```

所以：

```text
learn from mistakes
```

## 七、回译训练时的检查清单

写完英文后，按这个顺序检查：

```text
1. 介词后面是不是名词/动名词？
2. to 是介词还是不定式？
3. 动词是否已经自带宾语，不需要介词？例如 affect / discuss / enter / approach
4. 名词是否需要固定介词？例如 impact on / access to / lack of
5. 这个介词表达的是范围、方向、来源、工具、对象，还是目的？
```

记住：介词不是小装饰，它是英语句子里表达逻辑关系的骨架。
